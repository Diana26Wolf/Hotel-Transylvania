Hotel Transylvania

Let's meet the characters



Count Dracula

"I don't say bleh bleh bleh"
This is the classic line of the one and only Count Dracula. Everybody calls him Drac though. He has slick-black hair. He is Mavis Dracula's father. He is very protective of her. He is a really hilarious vampire and believes that human blood is too fat for his taste!

Mavis Dracula

"Holy Rabies"
This is the classic line of the one and only daughter of Count Dracula--MAVIS DRACULA! She has shoulder-length Raven black hair that has bangs hanging over her forehead. Mavis is an extremely cheerful vampire who also has an attitude. She was a pampered child, thanks to her father.

Jonathan Loughran

"I love your Dracula voice. It's so over the top!"
This was said by Jonathan Loughran who is Mavis's husband. People call him Johnny though. He is a human and has ginger hair. He is a talkitive and fun-loving human who arrived at Hotel Transylvania by accident and fell in love with Mavis.

Griffin

"Hey I have red hair!"
This was said by griffin, the invisible man. He is another friend of Dracula. He is really witty. His glasses are the only things that can be seen or maybe if he wears some other accessories. He is in love with Crystal, the invisible woman. He is a friend of Dracula. He can drive a car and play drums. He also forgets that he is invisible sometimes and expects people to know what he looks like.

Blobby

This guy can't talk. Blobby is a green, Jell-O-like blob monster. He is also Drac's friend.

Murray

"Here comes the party!"
This is Murray's line. He is a mummy. He is also Drac's best friend. He is a big-hearted guy. As a former entertainer to the great Pharaohs of Egypt, Murray is extremely likable, and always the center of every party. He can sing and dance really well.

Frankenstein

"He's my right arm cousin!"
This line was said by Frankenstein.Frank has light blue skin, green eyes, and black hair. He wears a blue suit with a white stripped shirt, red tie, and black boots. Comparing with humans, his limbs are relatively large. He is extremely powerful. He loves parties.

Wayne Werewolf

"What? Now there's no sheep on the road! Let's go!"
This line was said by Wayne Werewolf. He is the miserable father of hundreds of pups. They are absolutely savage and make his life Living Hell. He is an awesome DJ.
